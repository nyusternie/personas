The Cash Identity Problem

Traditional cash lacks digital identity infrastructure

Current Challenges

  • No way to recover lost/stolen cash
  • Limited traceability for legal cash
  • Missing ownership proof mechanisms
  • Vulnerable to counterfeiting

Digital Gap

  • Cash remains anonymous by design
  • No cryptographic verification
  • Limited integration with modern systems
  • Manual verification processes

Our Solution

Personas Cash brings crypto wallet-style security to physical cash

A blockchain-based Decentralized Identity (DID) system that enables:

  • Secure cash ownership verification
  • Recovery mechanisms for lost cash
  • Legal proof of legitimate ownership
  • QR-based verification system

How Personas Cash Works

  • 1. Generate DID

    Create unique decentralized identity for cash bundles

  • 2. Secure Storage

    Store DID proofs on decentralized blockchain

  • 3. QR Verification

    Scan QR codes to verify cash authenticity

  • 4. Recovery Option

    Recover lost cash through legal channels
